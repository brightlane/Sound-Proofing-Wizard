🔍 Top Soundproofing Questions

What is the best soundproofing material for walls?+

Mass Loaded Vinyl (MLV) is often used to add dense mass to walls and ceilings to help block airborne sound when properly installed as part of a layered system.

How much does soundproofing a room cost?+

Many home projects fall roughly in the $2–4 per square foot range for materials, with total cost depending on room size, chosen products, and how much work you do yourself.

Is MLV better than Green Glue?+

MLV mainly adds mass to resist airborne noise, while damping compounds between drywall layers help reduce vibration; combining mass and damping is a common strategy for higher isolation.
