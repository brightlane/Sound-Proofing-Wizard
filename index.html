🔍 Soundproofing FAQ (Top Questions Answered)

What is the best soundproofing material for walls?

MLV 1lb/sf blocks airborne noise best (STC 52). Combine with Green Glue for 60+ total.

How much does soundproofing a room cost?

$2-4/sf typical for DIY. A 12×10 room runs about $800–1600.

MLV vs Green Glue: Which is better?

MLV blocks sound; Green Glue damps vibration. Using both gives STC 65+ performance.

Can I soundproof my apartment?

✅ Yes. Use MLV with resilient channels for up to 25 dB noise reduction. Landlord‑friendly (no structural changes).

What STC rating do I need?

50–55 = normal speech quiet; 60+ = music & TV isolation. The calculator shows your boost.
